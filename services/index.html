    • What format are the photographs delivered in?
    We provide a full set of JPEG images taken with an 18-megapixel DSLR camera. They are delivered at a resolution of 5184 * 3456, which means they can be printed onto flyers and posters at great quality (prints are not included).

  • HDR (high-dynamic-range) imaging to capture more from brilliant sunlight to deep shadows
  • White balance and color temperature corrections done in post-processing
  • Additional sharpening, highlighting, and contrast to make each photo flawless
